Shipping Policy

We ship EKRPT hardware worldwide. This page explains how shipping works, what to expect, and how to track your order.

Where we ship

EKRPT Networking Labs ships worldwide. Whether you are in Nigeria, elsewhere in Africa, or further afield, we can get your order to you. Delivery times and costs vary by destination.

Processing time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment is confirmed. You will receive an email confirmation when your order is placed, and another when it ships.

Delivery times

Estimated delivery windows after dispatch:

  • Within Nigeria: typically 2–7 business days, depending on location.
  • Rest of Africa: typically 5–14 business days.
  • International: typically 7–21 business days, depending on destination and customs.

These are estimates, not guarantees. Customs processing, courier delays, and local conditions can affect delivery times.

Shipping costs

Shipping cost is calculated based on your destination and the size and weight of your order. The total, including any shipping fees, is shown clearly at checkout before you pay.

Customs, duties & taxes

For international orders, import duties, taxes, and customs fees may apply once your package reaches its destination country. These charges are set by your local authorities and are the customer's responsibility. We recommend checking your country's import rules before ordering.

Tracking your order

Once your order ships, we will email you the details. As your order moves through processing, shipping, and delivery, you will receive status updates. If you have questions about your shipment, contact us at office@ekrpt.com with your order number.

Need it urgently or shipping in bulk? For expedited delivery or B2B/bulk orders, contact our team and we will arrange the best option for you.
