Our Mission

Why EKRPT exists

EKRPT Networking Labs was founded with a single, clear mission: make elite-grade connectivity accessible to everyone. We believe strong network infrastructure is the backbone of modern business, gaming, and everyday life.

We source, brand, and distribute the world's best networking equipment — from our own EKRPT-branded routers and MiFi devices to official Starlink satellite hardware — and back every sale with technical expertise and long-term support.

Whether you are a competitive gamer, a home-office user, a growing enterprise, or an ISP expanding coverage, EKRPT is your trusted networking partner.

CA Michael

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

CA Michael founded EKRPT Networking Labs with a vision to build a globally competitive networking brand. With deep expertise in digital infrastructure, networking systems, and hardware distribution, he has built EKRPT into a brand known for quality, reliability, and after-sales support.

Under his leadership, EKRPT launched the SPECTRE WiFi 7 line and grew to distribute Starlink hardware including the Mini, Gen3, and V4 kits, alongside a proprietary line of EKRPT-branded routers and MiFi devices engineered for demanding real-world conditions.
