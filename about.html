Our Mission

Why EKRPT exists

EKRPT Networking Labs was founded with a single, clear mission: make reliable internet connectivity accessible to every Nigerian. We believe that strong network infrastructure is the backbone of modern business and everyday life.

We source, brand, and distribute the world's best networking equipment — from our own EKRPT-branded routers and MiFi devices to official Starlink satellite hardware — and back every sale with technical expertise and long-term support.

Whether you are a home user in Lagos, an enterprise in Abuja, or an ISP expanding coverage in a new region, EKRPT is your trusted networking partner.

CA Michael founded EKRPT Networking Labs with a vision to become Nigeria's most trusted networking equipment company. With deep expertise in digital infrastructure, networking systems, and hardware distribution, he has built EKRPT into a licensed and registered brand known for quality, reliability, and after-sales support.

Under his leadership, EKRPT has grown to distribute Starlink hardware including the Mini, Gen3, and V4 kits, alongside a proprietary line of EKRPT-branded routers and MiFi devices engineered specifically for Nigerian network conditions.
