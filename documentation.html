SPECTRE BE19000 ULTRA

Our flagship WiFi 7 tri-band gaming router. Key specifications:

  • WiFi standard: WiFi 7 (802.11be)
  • Bands: Tri-band (2.4 GHz / 5 GHz / 6 GHz)
  • Aggregate speed: up to 19,000 Mbps
  • Uplink: 10G SFP fibre-ready + 2.5G WAN
  • LAN: 3 × 1G Ethernet
  • Mobile: Dual nano-SIM with auto-switch (SA/NSA 5G; SIMs not included)
  • Antennas: 4 high-gain external antennas
  • Features: MLO, 4096-QAM, gaming QoS engine, USB port, mesh support
